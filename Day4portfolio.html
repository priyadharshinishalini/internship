Dark Mode Hero

Dark Mode Hero is the top section of a website designed with a dark background and light text to give a modern look. It usually includes a heading, description, and button to grab user attention. It helps improve visual appeal and user experience, especially in low-light viewing.

Centered Hero

Centered Mode is a layout style where content is placed at the center of a page (horizontally and/or vertically). It helps create a clean, balanced, and focused design. Commonly used in web pages like hero sections, forms, and landing pages.
